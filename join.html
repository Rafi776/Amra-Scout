Why You Join With Us?
Amra Scout Group is one of the best open scout groups in Bangladesh. Here you will get the chance to show your skill and creativity easily. You will get so many scopes to serve the country as well as the world. If want to make a positive change in society then ASG will always should be your first priority. Because we are always trying to make our society mentally and physically healthy. ASG have the highest number of active members. Here we are like a family. We are trying to work in disciplined way. Eligibility

Amra Scout Group currently recruits new members for Boy Scout and Rover section.

1. If you want to join as a boy scout then your age have to between 11 to 17.

2. If you want to join as a Rover then your age have to between 17 to 25
